Computational Immunology  ·  Co-first Author
ITGAP: Multimodal TCR–Antigen Prediction
Encoder-decoder integrating TCR sequences (Atchley factors + positional encoding) with batch-corrected single-cell transcriptomics. PR-AUC 0.821 on unseen TCRs, outperforming mvTCR (0.609) and Tessa (0.680). Predicted TAA-specific T cells enriched in long-term responders (p<0.05). Submitted to Nature Communications.

Smartphone Cough Acoustics for Airway Disease Screening
Three ML pipelines (full audio, segmented events, Google HeAR pretrained embeddings) to predict CXR abnormality across 1,058 adults in 5 countries undergoing TB evaluation. Best AUROC 0.822 with HeAR embeddings + demographics.

Cox PH, DeepSurv, Dynamic-DeepHit across 6,457 patients from 7 multinational RCTs. Integrating longitudinal PSA kinetics and genomic risk scores. Best C-index 0.794 vs. 0.654 clinical-only. Podium speaker at UCSF Prostate Cancer Retreat — only master's student invited.

Clinical Informatics  ·  UCSF Epic EHR
Antibiotic Timing & Neurosurgical Outcomes
Retrospective cohort of 826 elective adult neurosurgical cases built from scratch via SQL into de-identified UCSF Epic warehouse (2020–2022). Earlier antibiotic timing counterintuitively associated with longer operative duration — revealing surgical complexity confounding (Wilcoxon p<0.001).

Optimizing Team USA Gymnastics for Paris 2024
Simulation model using linear mixed effects regression across 168 athlete combinations from 39 competitions (109 countries, 1,917 individuals). Decomposed Simpson's Paradox in scoring data. Predicted Simone Biles-led women's team — aligned with actual Olympic selection.
