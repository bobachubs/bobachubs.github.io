Clinical AI & Fairness  ·  Stanford AI Ethics Capstone
LLM Extraction of SDOH for Outcome Prediction
Predicts major amputation or death within 1 year of revascularization in 4,821 CLTI patients from Stanford STARR (CPT-coded, 2006–present). Logistic regression, random forest, and gradient boosting achieve AUROC 0.743 for mortality; decision curve analysis confirms clinical utility across thresholds 0.05–0.35. Fairness analysis revealed race as the dominant inequity axis — equalized-odds difference ~0.75 — with threshold mitigation narrowing but not eliminating gaps. SDOH extraction from 600K+ clinical notes via LLM is the planned next phase.
